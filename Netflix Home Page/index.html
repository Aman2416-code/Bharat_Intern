Frequently Asked Questions

What is Netflix?

Netflix is a subscription video streaming service with over 238 million paid memberships. It offers a wide variety of TV shows, movies, anime, documentaries, and more. You can watch as much as you want, whenever you want, without a single commercial.

How much does Netflix cost?

Netflix offers three subscription plans:
Standard with Ads: $6.99 per month
Standard: $15.49 per month
Premium: $22.99 per month

Where can I watch?

You can watch Netflix on any internet-connected device that offers the Netflix app, including:
  • Smart TVs
  • Game consoles (Xbox, PlayStation, Nintendo Switch)
  • Streaming media players (Roku, Chromecast, Apple TV, Amazon Fire TV)
  • Set-top boxes (Xfinity X1, Cox Contour)
  • Blu-ray players
  • Smartphones and tablets
  • Computers

How do I cancel?

To cancel your Netflix subscription, follow these steps:
  1. Go to the Netflix website and sign in to your account.
  2. Click on the Profile icon in the top right corner of the screen.
  3. Click on Account.
  4. Under Membership & Billing, click on Cancel Membership.
  5. Click on Finish Cancellation to confirm.
Your cancellation will take effect at the end of your current billing period. You will still be able to watch Netflix until then.
If you are canceling your Netflix subscription on a mobile device:
  1. Open the Netflix app and tap on the More tab.
  2. Tap on Account.
  3. Under Membership & Billing, tap on Cancel Membership.
  4. Tap on Finish Cancellation to confirm.
If you are having trouble canceling your Netflix subscription, you can contact Netflix customer support for assistance.

Is Netflix good for kids?

Yes, Netflix can be good for kids, but it is important for parents to monitor their children's viewing and choose appropriate content. Netflix has a wide variety of kids' shows and movies, from educational and entertaining programs to preschool-friendly content. There is also a Kids profile that parents can create to limit their children's access to mature content.
